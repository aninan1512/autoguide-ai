Alternator Failure Symptoms

Common Symptoms

* Battery warning light on dashboard
* Dim headlights
* Electrical components malfunctioning
* Battery repeatedly dying

Possible Causes

* Worn alternator bearings
* Faulty voltage regulator
* Loose serpentine belt
* Damaged wiring

Diagnostic Steps

1. Test battery voltage with the engine running.
2. Inspect alternator belt tension.
3. Listen for unusual noises from the alternator.
4. Use a multimeter to verify charging voltage.

Expected charging voltage

* Typically between 13.7 and 14.7 volts.

When to visit a mechanic

* If alternator output is below recommended voltage.
