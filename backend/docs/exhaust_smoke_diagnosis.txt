Exhaust Smoke Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* Blue smoke from exhaust
* White smoke from exhaust
* Black smoke from exhaust

Possible Causes

Blue Smoke

* Burning engine oil
* Worn piston rings
* Valve seal leaks

White Smoke

* Coolant leak
* Blown head gasket

Black Smoke

* Excess fuel
* Faulty fuel injector
* Clogged air filter

Diagnostic Steps

1. Observe smoke color carefully.
2. Check engine oil level.
3. Inspect coolant level.
4. Scan vehicle for engine codes.
