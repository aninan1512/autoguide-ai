Engine Overheating Causes

Common Symptoms

* Temperature gauge rising
* Steam from engine bay
* Coolant warning light

Possible Causes

* Low coolant level
* Faulty thermostat
* Radiator blockage
* Broken water pump
* Cooling fan malfunction

Diagnostic Steps

1. Check coolant reservoir level.
2. Inspect radiator hoses for leaks.
3. Ensure cooling fans activate when engine temperature rises.
4. Inspect radiator cap condition.

Safety Notes

* Never open a hot radiator cap.
