Wheel Alignment Issues

Common Symptoms

* Vehicle pulling to one side
* Uneven tire wear
* Steering wheel off-center

Possible Causes

* Improper wheel alignment
* Worn suspension components
* Tire pressure imbalance

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect tire pressure levels.
2. Check tire tread wear patterns.
3. Perform wheel alignment using alignment machine.

When to visit a mechanic

* Alignment requires professional equipment.
