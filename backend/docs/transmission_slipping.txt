Transmission Slipping Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* Engine revs increase but vehicle does not accelerate
* Delayed gear engagement
* Harsh gear shifts

Possible Causes

* Low transmission fluid
* Worn clutch plates
* Faulty transmission solenoid
* Transmission fluid contamination

Diagnostic Steps

1. Check transmission fluid level and condition.
2. Inspect transmission for leaks.
3. Scan vehicle for transmission error codes.
4. Test drive vehicle to observe shifting behavior.

When to visit a mechanic

* Transmission repairs require specialized tools.
