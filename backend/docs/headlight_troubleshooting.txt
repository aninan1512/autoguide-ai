Headlight Troubleshooting Guide

Common Symptoms

* One headlight not working
* Both headlights not turning on
* Flickering headlights
* Dim headlights

Possible Causes

* Burned out bulb
* Blown fuse
* Faulty relay
* Wiring damage
* Faulty headlight switch

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect the headlight bulb for visible damage.
2. Check the headlight fuse in the fuse box.
3. Swap the relay with a known working relay if possible.
4. Inspect wiring near the headlight housing.
5. Test the headlight switch for proper operation.

Tools Needed

* Screwdriver
* Multimeter
* Replacement bulb

Safety Notes

* Disconnect battery before working on wiring.
* Avoid touching halogen bulbs directly with bare hands.
