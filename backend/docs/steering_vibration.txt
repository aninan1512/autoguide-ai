Steering Wheel Vibration Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* Steering wheel shaking while driving
* Vibration during braking
* Uneven tire wear

Possible Causes

* Unbalanced tires
* Warped brake rotors
* Worn suspension components
* Bent wheel rim

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect tires for uneven wear.
2. Balance wheels.
3. Inspect suspension bushings and joints.
4. Check brake rotors for warping.

When to visit a mechanic

* If suspension components are damaged.
