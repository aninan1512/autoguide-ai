Car Won't Start Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* Engine does not crank
* Clicking noise when starting
* Engine cranks but does not start

Possible Causes

* Dead battery
* Faulty starter motor
* Bad ignition switch
* Fuel pump failure
* Empty fuel tank

Diagnostic Steps

1. Check battery voltage.
2. Listen for clicking noises indicating starter issues.
3. Ensure the fuel pump activates when the key is turned.
4. Check fuses related to starting systems.
5. Inspect starter motor connections.

Safety Notes

* Always ensure the vehicle is in park or neutral before testing.
