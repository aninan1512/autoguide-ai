Fuse and Relay Troubleshooting

Common Symptoms

* Electrical components not working
* Headlights or horn not functioning
* Power windows failing

Possible Causes

* Blown fuse
* Faulty relay
* Wiring short circuit
* Loose electrical connection

Diagnostic Steps

1. Locate the fuse box using the vehicle manual.
2. Inspect fuses for broken metal strips.
3. Replace blown fuses with correct amperage.
4. Swap relays with identical relays for testing.
5. Inspect wiring for damage or corrosion.

Safety Notes

* Always turn off the ignition before replacing fuses.
