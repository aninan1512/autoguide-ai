Fuel Pump Failure Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* Engine cranks but will not start
* Loss of power while driving
* Whining noise from fuel tank

Possible Causes

* Failed fuel pump
* Clogged fuel filter
* Electrical issues

Diagnostic Steps

1. Listen for fuel pump sound when key is turned.
2. Check fuel pressure with gauge.
3. Inspect fuel pump fuse and relay.

When to visit a mechanic

* If fuel pressure is below recommended levels.
