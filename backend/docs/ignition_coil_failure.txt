Ignition Coil Failure Guide

Common Symptoms

* Engine misfires
* Rough idle
* Reduced fuel efficiency
* Check engine light

Possible Causes

* Worn ignition coil
* Faulty spark plugs
* Damaged ignition wires

Diagnostic Steps

1. Scan vehicle for OBD codes.
2. Inspect spark plugs.
3. Swap ignition coils to isolate faulty units.

Tools Needed

* OBD scanner
* Socket wrench
