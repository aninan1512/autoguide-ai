Suspension Noise Troubleshooting

Common Symptoms

* Clunking sound when driving over bumps
* Vehicle bouncing excessively
* Uneven tire wear

Possible Causes

* Worn shock absorbers
* Loose suspension bolts
* Damaged control arm bushings
* Faulty sway bar links

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect shocks and struts for leaks.
2. Check suspension components for looseness.
3. Inspect sway bar links for wear.
4. Test vehicle bounce response.

Safety Notes

* Suspension repairs may require professional tools.
