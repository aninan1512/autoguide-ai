Cooling System Troubleshooting

Common Symptoms

* Engine overheating
* Coolant leaks
* Heater not producing heat

Possible Causes

* Low coolant level
* Failed thermostat
* Radiator blockage
* Faulty water pump
* Cooling fan malfunction

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect coolant reservoir level.
2. Check radiator hoses for leaks or cracks.
3. Verify cooling fan operation.
4. Inspect radiator for debris blockage.
5. Test thermostat functionality.

Safety Notes

* Never remove radiator cap while engine is hot.
