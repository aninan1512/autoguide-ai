Brake Noise Diagnosis Guide

Common Symptoms

* Squeaking noise while braking
* Grinding noise
* Vibrations during braking
* Reduced braking performance

Possible Causes

* Worn brake pads
* Contaminated brake pads
* Warped brake rotors
* Loose brake components

Diagnostic Steps

1. Inspect brake pads for wear.
2. Check rotor surface for grooves or damage.
3. Ensure caliper bolts are secure.
4. Clean brake components with brake cleaner.
5. Replace worn brake pads or rotors.

Safety Notes

* Brake systems are critical for vehicle safety.
* If unsure about repairs, consult a professional mechanic.

When to visit a mechanic

* If grinding noises persist.
* If braking performance decreases significantly.
