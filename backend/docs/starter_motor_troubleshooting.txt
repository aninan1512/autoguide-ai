Starter Motor Troubleshooting Guide

Common Symptoms

* Engine does not crank when turning the key
* Single clicking noise when starting
* Starter spins but engine does not engage

Possible Causes

* Faulty starter motor
* Weak battery
* Bad starter solenoid
* Corroded battery cables
* Faulty ignition switch

Diagnostic Steps

1. Check battery voltage before testing the starter.
2. Listen for clicking sounds from the starter solenoid.
3. Inspect wiring connections at the starter motor.
4. Tap the starter lightly with a tool while attempting to start.
5. Test voltage at the starter terminals.

Tools Needed

* Multimeter
* Socket wrench
* Battery tester

When to visit a mechanic

* If starter replacement is required.
