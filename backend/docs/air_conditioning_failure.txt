Air Conditioning Failure Diagnosis

Common Symptoms

* AC blowing warm air
* Weak airflow
* Strange noises from compressor

Possible Causes

* Low refrigerant level
* Faulty AC compressor
* Clogged cabin air filter
* Electrical issue in AC system

Diagnostic Steps

1. Check cabin air filter condition.
2. Inspect AC compressor clutch operation.
3. Check refrigerant pressure levels.
4. Inspect AC system fuses and relays.

When to visit a mechanic

* AC refrigerant systems require specialized equipment.
